Venue

The meeting will take place in the city of Cádiz. It is hosted by the Universidad de Cádiz and will be held at the Sala Tomasa Palafox in the mornings (first floor of Edificio Constitución 1812 ) and the Sala de Juntas 4 after lunch (second floor of the Annex 2 of the Facultad de Filosofía y Letras).
